Between December 2 and December 7, I will attend [EurIPS 2025](https://eurips.cc/) in Copenhagen, an event officially endorsed by [NeurIPS](https://neurips.cc/). 
I will present two papers there: "[Benchmarking Optimizers for Large Language Model Pretraining](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01440)" and "[Just a Simple Transformation is Enough for Data Protection in Split Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.11689)".
In addition, I will give presentations at both the workshop on the [Science of Benchmarking and Evaluating AI](https://sites.google.com/view/benchmarking-and-evaluating-ai) and the [Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning](https://ppml-eurips-2025.github.io/) workshop.
