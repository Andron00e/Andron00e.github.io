A [scholarship](https://www.ai.uzh.ch/en/hot-topics/news/2026/UZH.ai-x-DSI-Excellence-Program.html) given to the top 6 incoming / first-year PhD students at the University of Zurich. Paid as a contribution of **$60000** toward a PhD salary (for one year only). Also includes personal research expenses budget of **CHF 3000** in flexible funding for international conferences or research equipment. Fellows are fully integrated into the interdisciplinary [DSI Excellence Program](https://www.dsi.uzh.ch/en/education/excellence-program.html) curriculum.
